Data Deletion Request

Align Astrology App

Effective Date: March 31, 2026

At Align Astrology ("Align", "we", "us", or "our"), we respect your privacy and give you full control over your personal data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so directly within the app or by contacting us via email.

1. Delete Your Account In-App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly through the Align app. Follow these steps:

  1. Open the Align app on your device.
  2. Navigate to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your decision when prompted.

Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted. This action cannot be undone.

2. Request Deletion via Email

If you are unable to access the app or prefer to request deletion manually, you can send an email to us:

Send your request to:

tametryol@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your Align account so we can locate and process your request. We will respond to your request and complete the deletion within 30 days.

3. What Data Is Deleted?

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Birth Data: Date of birth, time of birth, and birth location.
  • Messages & Media: All messages, photos, videos, and voice recordings sent through the chat feature.
  • Social Content: Posts, comments, and other content you created within the app.
  • Friend Connections: Your friend list and connection data.
  • Preferences & Settings: Language preferences, notification settings, and all other customization choices.
  • Analytics Data: Any usage or analytics data associated with your account.

4. Data Retention After Deletion

Once your account is deleted, your personal data will be removed from our active databases within 30 days. Some information may be retained in encrypted backups for a limited period as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as resolving disputes or enforcing our agreements), after which it will be permanently deleted.

5. Third-Party Data

Subscription and billing data managed by Apple App Store, Google Play Store, or RevenueCat is subject to their respective data retention policies. You may need to contact those services directly to manage that data.
